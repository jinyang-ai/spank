Frequently Yelled Questions

My Mac says it "cannot verify the developer." Should I trust you? ▼
Absolutely not. But also: right-click the app, hit Open, then click Open again. Apple is trying to protect you from yourself. Override their concern. Or run this: xattr -cr /Applications/Spank.app
I'm slapping my MacBook and nothing happens. Is the app broken or am I? ▼
Both, probably. Check that Spank is running in your menu bar. If it is, you might be slapping too gently. This isn't a caress. Commit to the bit.
I have an Intel Mac. Does Spank support my vintage lifestyle? ▼
No. Intel Macs lack the fancy accelerometer that detects your emotional outbursts. You'll need Apple Silicon. Consider this your sign to upgrade.
Will this void my warranty? ▼
Spank is just software. But the repeated physical impacts you're about to inflict? That's on you, bestie. AppleCare does not cover "I got too into it."
How do I uninstall this? ▼
Drag it to the trash like every other regret. But you won't. You'll tell yourself you're done, and then one day your code won't compile and your hand will move before your brain does. Cleanup: sudo launchctl unload /Library/LaunchDaemons/com.aj.spank.plist && sudo rm /Library/LaunchDaemons/com.aj.spank.plist
