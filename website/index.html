Questions

Why does it need my password?
The accelerometer requires root access because Apple doesn't want you slapping your $3000 laptop. We're not mining crypto or stealing your nudes—just detecting violence. It's open source. Trust issues? Read the code.
Apple says "unidentified developer"
Yeah, Apple doesn't trust software that makes your laptop moan when you hit it. Can't imagine why. Right-click → Open → Open again. Or if you're feeling terminal:
xattr -cr /Applications/Spank.app
I slapped it and nothing happened
First, look for the 🍑 in your menu bar. If it's there, you're not slapping hard enough. This isn't a gentle caress—commit to the violence. Really smack it. Your laptop can take it. Probably.
Will this break my MacBook?
Look, the accelerometer exists to detect drops. We're just... finding alternative uses. Apple engineers did not anticipate this. That said, we are absolutely not liable for cracked screens, dented chassis, or HR complaints. Spank responsibly.
My coworkers are staring at me
Good. Assert dominance. Maintain eye contact while slapping your laptop. If they ask questions, just say "stress relief" and slap harder. This is a conversation ender, not a conversation starter.
Intel Mac?
No accelerometer on Intel. Apple said "you don't need to know when your laptop is being abused" and honestly they were wrong. M1/M2/M3/M4 only. Consider this your sign to upgrade.
How do I uninstall this chaos?
Regretting your life choices? Click the 🍑 → Stop Daemon, then drag to trash. For the nuclear option:
sudo launchctl unload /Library/LaunchDaemons/com.aj.spank.plist && rm -rf /Applications/Spank.app
Can I add my ex's voicemail as a sound?
Absolutely. Drop any .mp3 into the sounds folder and it shows up in the picker. We won't judge. Actually, we might judge a little. But the app won't.
